Welcome! I'm Donald Willcox and I'm a graduate research assistant in computational nuclear astrophysics at Stony Brook University working with Prof. Alan Calder. My current research interests include simulations of Type Ia Supernovae from novel progenitor systems and multidimensional reactive-hydrodynamic studies of the Urca process in Type Ia progenitors.

For information about my research, see my CV and Research pages.
